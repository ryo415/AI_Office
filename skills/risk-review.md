# Risk Review Skill

## Purpose

Identify risks, assumptions, and missing information.

## Input

- Draft
- Goal
- Intended use
- Constraints

## Rules

- Prioritize material risks.
- Be clear about impact.
- Suggest concrete mitigations.
- Do not block progress for minor concerns.
- Separate confirmed risks from assumptions.

## Output

```markdown
## Risk Summary

## High Risks

## Medium Risks

## Low Risks

## Assumptions

## Missing Information

## Suggested Fixes
```
